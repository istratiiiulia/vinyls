Description

The Scorpions celebrated their 60th band anniversary with a special arena show in the city where it all began – Hannover, Germany. On July 5, 2025, they performed their most iconic songs from 60 years of band history under the motto, “Coming Home,” accompanied by special guests Alice Cooper, Judas Priest and more. The recording of this monumental, sold-out show is available as a live album – for the 45,000 fans who experienced this historical moment in person and for fans all over the world who want to celebrate with the band and relive their own memories from the last six decades.
